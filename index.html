About MARS

Welcome to the Metropolitan Aerospace Rocketry Society (MARS) - your gateway to the world of aerospace and rocketry. We are a passionate team dedicated to exploring the skies and beyond. Whether you're a seasoned rocket scientist or just curious about the cosmos, MARS has something for you!

Our Mission

At MARS, our mission is to foster interest and knowledge in aerospace, rocketry, and space exploration. We aim to inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and space enthusiasts.
